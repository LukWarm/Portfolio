About Me

Hello! My name is Egan and I enjoy creating robots and working on cars. My interest in mechanics started back in 2013 when I joined my school's LEGO robotics team. Who knew playing with LEGOs would teach me a lot about DFM & FMEA!

Fast-forward to today, and I've had the privilege of applying my skills designing robots and documenting the process for competition, going as far as winning the Inspire Award in the state of Georgia in 2018 with my team, and being nominated by my coaches as a Dean's List semi-finalist 2020 and 2021.

My main focus these days is learning and pursuing a Mechanical Engineering Degree and building competitive, reliable robots and mechanical systems at Ultraviolet Robotics with my teammates.

Here are a few things I've been working with recently:

  • Autodesk Inventor
  • Mechanical Drawings
  • Problem Solving
  • Precision Metrology
  • 3D Printing
  • CNC Machining
